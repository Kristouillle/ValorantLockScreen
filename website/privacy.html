Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

What this app does

Valorant Lock Screen displays live results of tracked Valorant matches on iPhone widgets and Live Activities.

Information collected

The app stores your tracked team selections and backend configuration needed to fetch match data. The service may also store technical push registration tokens required to update widgets and Live Activities.

No personal information is collected beyond what is necessary for the app to function, and even your preferences are stored locally on your device.

I do not use any database, analytics or tracking services.

How information is used

Data is used only to provide match tracking, widget refreshes, Live Activity updates, service reliability, and debugging.

Third-party services

Match information may be sourced from Riot-owned esports data surfaces or approved upstream services used to operate the product.

While I await approval to use the Riot API, I currently source match schedule from the valorantesports.com website. Score and results are currently using mock data provided by my simulation backend.
